OPEN WINDOWS TO LEARNING

Nearly 40,000 local students are inspired every year through the Festival's year-round education outreach program. The Festival works hard to reach all students, regardless of their ability to pay, through in-school performances, master classes, student matinees, and our original Artists in Residence, the Rhythm Project.

Your gift can be the one to inspire a child today.
Donate to Education Outreach

AN IN-DEPTH LOOK AT OUR COMMUNITY'S ARTISTS IN RESIDENCE

The Rhythm Project is the cornerstone of Virginia Arts Festival's year round education program. More than 200 local students participate in this program each year. The results are tried and true: nearly 99% of students who have participated in the Rhythm Project All-Stars have graduated from high school and gone on to college or joined the military.
